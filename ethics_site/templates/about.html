About Ethics in Tech

Welcome to Ethics in Tech — a platform dedicated to exploring the intersection of technology, artificial intelligence, and ethics.

Our goal is to provide thought-provoking resources, articles, videos, and a vibrant community where people can discuss the ethical implications of modern technologies.

Our Mission

We aim to foster a space where individuals, professionals, and researchers can collaborate to promote ethical practices in the design, development, and deployment of technology.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Articles on ethics in AI, technology, and business.
  • Curated videos discussing ethical dilemmas and solutions.
  • A community section to engage in thoughtful conversations.
